About Us

The Library of New Jersey is West Orange's premier cannabis dispensary, offering a curated selection of premium cannabis products for both medical and recreational customers. Our expert budtenders provide personalized recommendations and education to help you find the perfect products for your needs.

Founded on principles of community, education, equality, and equity, we're proud to be one of New Jersey's first Black-owned cannabis dispensaries.

Legal Information

New Jersey Cannabis License: Class 5 Retail

Age Requirement: Must be 21+ with valid government-issued ID

Medical Patients: 18+ with valid NJ medical marijuana card
